Description

This comprehensive final exam covers all major topics in data structures including arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, graphs, and sorting algorithms. The exam consists of both theoretical questions and practical implementation problems. Students are expected to demonstrate understanding of time complexity analysis, algorithm design, and efficient problem-solving techniques. This material is particularly useful for students preparing for their final examinations or those looking to review key concepts in data structures.
